Squominoes

General Rules:

  1. Shuffle the deck and deal 6 cards to each player.
  2. Place the rest of the deck face down.
  3. The youngest player goes first and plays a Squominoe card.
  4. Pick up a card from the deck after you play one.
  5. Players take turns connecting their cards to the ones on the table (clockwise).
  6. The goal is to get rid of as many cards as possible.

Special Cases:

  • If you can't play, pick up cards until you can.
  • If the table is full, add up the numbers on your remaining cards. The player with the lowest total wins.
  • If a player has no cards left, they win!

Play Area Size (depends on players):

  • 2 Players - 3x3 grid
  • 3 Players - 4x4 grid
  • 4 Players - 5x5 grid
  • and so on...
