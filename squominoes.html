Squominoes

General Rules:

  1. Deal: Each player gets 6 cards.
  2. Start the Game: The first player places a card on the table.
  3. Take Turns: Players take turns placing a card that connects to the last card played. If you can connect to more than one card, you may play an extra card for each connection.
  4. Draw a Card: After each turn, draw a card from the deck.
  5. Can't Play?: If you can't play a card, draw one and skip your turn.
  6. Winning: The first player to get rid of all their cards wins.
  7. Tiebreaker: If no one can play, the player with the fewest points on their remaining cards wins.
