Squominoes


General Rules:


  • Shuffle the deck.
  • Deal each player six (6) cards.
  • Place the remaining cards face down to the side.
  • The youngest player places a Squominoe first.
  • Once you play a card, don't forget to pick one up from the deck.
  • Going clockwise, the next player will connect one of their cards to a card on the table.
  • The goal is to try and get rid of as many of your cards as possible.

  • If you can't play any Squominoes, keep picking up from the deck until you can.
  • If the play area is full, total up all of the numbers on every one of your cards, the lowest number wins.
  • If a player has no cards left in their hand, they win.
  • The play area is determined by the amount of players...

    • 2 Players - 3 x 3 grid
    • 3 Players - 4 x 4 grid
    • 4 Players - 5 x 5 grid
    • and so on...
