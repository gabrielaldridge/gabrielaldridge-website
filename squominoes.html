Squominoes

General Rules:

  1. Each player is dealt 6 cards.
  2. One player starts by placing a card on the table.
  3. Players take turns, placing a card that connects to the last card played.
  4. If a player connects to more than one card, they can play one extra card for each connection.
  5. After each turn, draw a card from the deck.
  6. If a player canâ€™t play, they draw a card and skip their turn.
  7. The first player to get rid of all their cards wins.
  8. If no one can play, the player with the fewest total points on their cards wins.
