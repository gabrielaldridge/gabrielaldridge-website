Gabriel's Auditory Playground

Gabriel's Auditory Playground is my final submission at university. I want to make a game with no visuals, that will be as accessible for the visually impaired as it is for anyone else.

Dev Log - 13/03/22

At this point of the development cycle, the first minigame is in its prototyping phase. I've added a Shephard Tone that increases in volume depending on the distance between the player and the closest enemy.
